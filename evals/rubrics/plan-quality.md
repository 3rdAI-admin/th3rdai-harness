# Rubric: Plan Quality

Score each category from 1 to 5.

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Missing or unusable |
| 2 | Weak and requires major revision |
| 3 | Usable with gaps |
| 4 | Strong with minor gaps |
| 5 | Excellent and implementation-ready |

## Criteria

- Goal clarity
- Scope control
- Context accuracy
- Assumption handling
- Implementation feasibility
- Validation specificity
- Risk and edge-case coverage
- File impact accuracy
- Handoff clarity

## Pass Threshold

A plan passes when:

- No criterion scores below 3.
- Average score is 4 or higher.
- Validation criteria are concrete enough for a builder or evaluator to execute.
